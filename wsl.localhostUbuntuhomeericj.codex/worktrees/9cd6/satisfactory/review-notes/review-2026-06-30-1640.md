### Major

Verified live: `getComputedStyle(:root)['--accent']` = `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)` (the neutral surface), not orange.
  - Affected consumers reading `var(--accent)` for an intended orange accent: [`src/features/calculator/CalculatorControls.tsx:52`](src/features/calculator/CalculatorControls.tsx) (Segmented active), [`src/components/ui/switch.tsx:13`](src/components/ui/switch.tsx) (checked), [`src/components/ui/checkbox.tsx:14`](src/components/ui/checkbox.tsx) (checked), [`src/components/ui/tabs.tsx:30`](src/components/ui/tabs.tsx) + [`src/routes/data/route.tsx:27`](src/routes/data/route.tsx) (active tab border), [`src/components/ui/panel.tsx:35`](src/components/ui/panel.tsx) (top rail), [`src/components/shell/Sidebar.tsx:52-53`](src/components/shell/Sidebar.tsx) (logo icon + dot) and `:77` (active rail), [`src/features/calculator/ProductionGraph.tsx:62`](src/features/calculator/ProductionGraph.tsx), [`src/components/ui/badge.tsx:36`](src/components/ui/badge.tsx) (accent tone).
  - **Type:** bug
  - **Fix direction:** Resolve the clash at the token level, per the design spec's documented risk ([design spec line 150](docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-29-ficsit-design-system-foundation-design.md)). FICSIT's raw `--accent` should remain orange; shadcn's neutral-hover need should point at a separate variable. Concretely: stop redefining raw `--accent` to `--surface-hover` at `styles.css:29`, and instead map shadcn's `@theme inline` `--color-accent` / `--accent-foreground` (used by shadcn `bg-accent`/`text-accent-foreground` utilities, `styles.css:64-65`) to `--surface-hover` / `--text-primary` directly — so raw `var(--accent)` stays orange while shadcn's neutral-hover utilities still resolve to the neutral surface. Verify no shadcn primitive that relied on the neutral `bg-accent` regresses to orange.
  - **Acceptance:** `getComputedStyle(document.documentElement).getPropertyValue('--accent')` resolves to the FICSIT orange (`#fa9549`). The Calculator "Solve Mode" selected segment is orange with readable dark text; switches/checkboxes show orange when on; active tab indicators, panel top rails, and the sidebar logo/active rail are orange.
